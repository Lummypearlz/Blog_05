What is 3D Animation? Everything You Should Know

11 May 2021

It talks about the processes of 3D animation.3D animation is a popular and widely used style in various industries, including entertainment, education, medicine, architecture, and advertising. Its appeal lies in its excitement, engagement, and ability to communicate effectively. Businesses rely on 3D animations for brand storytelling, image building, and customer attraction. This article provides an overview of 3D animation, its process, best software, and tips for creating animations online, even for non-pro animators.2D animation is flat, based on a two-dimensional plane with x and y axes, like Pinocchio, Aladdin, and The Lion King. 3D animation, with an additional axis, adds depth and makes animations more realistic.
