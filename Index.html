What Is 3D Animation? Types, Processes, and Uses

21 Dec 2021

The method of producing moving three-dimensional visuals in a computer environment is known as 3D animation. Animators can make digital things that appear 3D even though they are on a 2D surface thanks to the use of 3D software in the creation of these images. Animators may create the illusion of three-dimensional movement in anything from a car in an advertisement to a character in a video game with timing and visual effects.
